Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

The Short Version

We don't collect, store, or share any of your data. Period.

What Data We Collect

None. Zero. Nothing.

TwoCamSwing is designed to work entirely on your devices without sending any information to us or any third parties.

Camera & Microphone

The app requires access to your device's camera and microphone to function:

  • Camera: Used to capture and stream golf swing video between your devices
  • Microphone: Used to detect the sound of a golf ball being struck (for automatic replay trigger)

All video and audio stays on your local devices. Nothing is uploaded, transmitted to the internet, or stored on external servers.

Local Network

The app uses your local Wi-Fi network to connect two iOS devices directly to each other. This connection is peer-to-peer and does not route through any external servers.

Data Storage

Any swing recordings you save are stored locally on your device only. We have no access to them.

Analytics & Tracking

We don't use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services. No third-party SDKs phone home.

Children's Privacy

Since we collect no data, there's nothing to worry about here either.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy (unlikely, since there's nothing to change), we'll update this page.
