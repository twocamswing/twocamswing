Face-On Camera (Front View)

Position directly in front of the golfer, aligned with the ball. This view shows:

  • Weight shift and balance
  • Hip and shoulder rotation
  • Head movement
  • Club face at impact
Wide-Angle Lens Recommended: At 6-8 feet distance, the standard iPhone camera may not capture the full swing. A clip-on wide-angle lens (0.6x or wider) solves this. Available for under $20 on Amazon - search for "iPhone wide angle clip lens".

Down-the-Line Camera (Side View)

Position behind the golfer, looking down the target line. This view shows:

  • Swing plane
  • Club path
  • Posture and spine angle
  • Extension through impact

App Setup

1

Install on Both Devices

Download TwoCamSwing from the App Store on both iOS devices.

2

Connect to Same WiFi

Ensure both devices are connected to the same WiFi network. The app uses local peer-to-peer networking - no internet required, but same network is essential.

3

Start the Sender (Camera Device)

On the device that will be the down-the-line camera, tap "Start as Sender". This device streams video to the receiver.

4

Start the Receiver (Viewing Device)

On the device where you'll view both angles (typically the face-on position), tap "Start as Receiver". This device shows both camera feeds side-by-side.

5

Grant Permissions

Allow camera, microphone, and local network access when prompted. The microphone is used for sound-triggered capture.

Pro Tip: Position the receiver (iPad works great) where the golfer or coach can easily see the replay after each swing.

Using the App

1

Automatic Capture

Just swing! The app listens for the impact sound and automatically triggers a slow-motion replay. No buttons to press.

2

Manual Replay

Tap the replay button anytime to see the last few seconds in slow motion.

3

Draw Lines

Draw reference lines on either video by touching and dragging. Use the clear button to remove lines.

4

Review in Library

All swings are automatically saved. Access the video library from the menu to review past swings at any speed.

5

Adjust Settings

Access settings from the menu to adjust slow-motion speed (0.15x, 0.25x, 0.5x) and replay repeat count.
